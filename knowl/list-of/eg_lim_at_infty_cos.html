Example 8.6. Limit at infinity: \(\cos\).

Let \(f(x)=\cos x\text{.}\)
  1. Provide a graph of \(f\) over its entire domain.
  2. Argue graphically whether the infinite limits \(\lim\limits_{x\to \pm \infty}f(x)\) exist.
Solution.
