Theorem 20.3 (Fermat). Critical points and extreme values.

Assume \(f\) is continuous on \(D\) and that \(a\) is an interior point of \(D\text{.}\) If \(f(a)\) is a local maximum or minimum value of \(f\) on \(D\text{,}\) and if \(f\) is differentiable at \(a\text{,}\) then \(f'(a)=0\text{.}\)
